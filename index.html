who i am
I design things that feel beautiful and genuinely work.

Hi — I'm Aroosha Rehman, a UI/UX designer based in New York with a deep belief that the best experiences are the ones that make you feel seen.

Design, to me, is deeply personal. It's the feeling you get when something just works — when it looks exactly right and moves exactly right and makes you feel something. That pursuit of feeling is what drives everything I make.

I've designed end-to-end products from research through high-fidelity prototypes. I care about craft, empathy, and making things that are genuinely beautiful and useful at the same time.
